DISC Test + Spiritual Gifts

About this Test

This test will help determine your personality type and guide you towards your spiritual gifts.

You will be asked 92 short questions. Each question will have five possible answers:

  • Never - This is hardly ever true for me or this does not apply to me.
  • Rarely - This is rarely true for me or this rarely applies to me.
  • Sometimes - This is sometimes true for me or this sometimes applies to me.
  • Often - This is often true for me or this often applies to me.
  • Always - This is almost always true for me or this almost always applies to me.
